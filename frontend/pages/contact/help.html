Sure Bhanu 💙 Here is a simple and clear point-wise User Guide for your E-Commerce Website: --- User guide Website 1️⃣ Home Page Displays featured products and offers. Shows categories (Electronics, Fashion, etc.). Search bar available at the top. Navigation menu for easy browsing. --- 2️⃣ User Registration Click on Sign Up. Enter name, email, mobile number, and password. Account created successfully. --- 3️⃣ User Login Click on Login. Enter registered email and password. Click Submit to access your account. --- 4️⃣ Browse Products Select category from menu. Use filters (price, rating, brand). Click on product to view details. --- 5️⃣ Product Details Page View product images and description. Check price and availability. Read customer reviews. Click Add to Cart or Buy Now. --- 6️⃣ Add to Cart View selected items. Update quantity if needed. Remove items if required. Click Proceed to Checkout. --- 7️⃣ Checkout & Payment Enter shipping address. Choose payment method: Credit/Debit Card UPI Net Banking Cash on Delivery Complete payment. Receive order confirmation. --- 8️⃣ Order Tracking Go to My Orders. Click on order details. Track delivery status. --- 9️⃣ Logout Click on profile icon. Select Logout. Account session ends securely.
