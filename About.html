About Us

Sri Manjunatha Shamiyana Works & Events is a trusted name in event management, tent house services, and decorative solutions. We celebrate the beauty of Indian art and design while building vibrant cultural connections across India and beyond. Weddings and cultural events reflect deep traditions and values. We provide creative, high-quality, end-to-end event solutions that enhance these traditions while promoting Indian culture on a wider platform.

Our focus is on quality, customer needs, and promoting Indian values makes it a great choice for those who want to incorporate traditional Indian designs into their wedding or event decor.

Founder

Narsimha BN

Narsimha BN is a professional event management expert with extensive experience in organizing weddings, cultural programs, and grand events. He is a supplier and manufacturer of complete tent house and decoration items.

He is a dedicated and experienced Event Manager known for his excellent planning, creativity, and co-ordination skills. He manages events with great attention in detail and ensures that every program is organized smoothly and successfully. He handles all aspects of event like decoration and lighting. With a strong focus on creativity, quality, and timely execution, he delivers elegant and memorable events tailored to client needs.

Over the years, he has built a strong reputation for reliability, transparent communication, and customer satisfaction, making him a trusted choice for events of all scales.
